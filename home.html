OUR HISTORY

Serenity Woods was founded in 2005 by a group of nature enthusiasts who wanted to share the beauty of the forest with the world. Since then, we have been providing a unique experience for our guests, combining the tranquility of nature with the comfort of modern amenities. Our mission is to create a peaceful retreat where guests can relax, rejuvenate, and reconnect with nature.
