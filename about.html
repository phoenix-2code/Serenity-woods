About Serenity Woods

Discover the history and mission of Serenity Woods.

Our History

Founded in 2005 by a group of nature enthusiasts, Serenity Woods has grown into a peaceful retreat that blends the tranquility of nature with modern comfort. Our mission is to provide a serene environment for guests to relax, rejuvenate, and reconnect with nature.

Milestones

"Embrace the Serenity, Live the Moment"

2005

Serenity Woods was established, welcoming its first guests.

2010

Opened the first wellness and spa services, offering a unique blend of relaxation and nature.

2020

Introduced event services for weddings, corporate events, and celebrations.
